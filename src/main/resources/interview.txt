
## LoadRunner Enterprise — Advanced

**Architecture & Design**

1. How have you designed an end-to-end performance testing framework from scratch? What were your key design decisions around script structure, reusability, and maintainability?
2. How do you architect a LoadRunner Enterprise setup for a large enterprise program with multiple applications, teams, and parallel test executions?
3. Have you worked with LoadRunner Enterprise's MI Listener and Unified Controller? How have you managed cross-geography distributed load generation at scale?
4. How do you manage LoadRunner Enterprise in a shared services model — where multiple project teams are consuming the same infrastructure?

**Scripting Depth**

5. How do you handle complex authentication mechanisms like OAuth2.0, SAML, or certificate-based auth in LoadRunner scripts?
6. Have you written custom C-based functions or used external libraries within VuGen? Give an example of a complex scripting challenge you solved.
7. How do you handle WebSocket, gRPC, or async API protocols in LoadRunner? What are the limitations you've encountered?
8. What is your approach to handling highly dynamic applications (SPAs, heavy JavaScript rendering) where traditional correlation breaks down?

**Execution & Stability**

9. How do you ensure test repeatability and environment stability across multiple test cycles? What pre-test checklists or gates do you enforce?
10. How do you handle flaky or inconsistent test results? Walk me through your debugging methodology.
11. At what point do you decide a performance test environment is "production-like enough" to trust the results?

**Analysis & Root Cause**

12. Walk me through the most complex performance bottleneck you've ever diagnosed. What tools and data points led you to the root cause?
13. How do you differentiate between an application-level bottleneck, a database bottleneck, a network issue, and an infrastructure constraint purely from LoadRunner and server-side data?
14. How do you build a performance baseline and use it to detect regression across releases?

## AWS — Advanced

**Infrastructure at Scale**

15. How have you architected a cloud-based load generation setup on AWS to simulate 50,000+ concurrent virtual users? What were the challenges?
16. How do you handle AWS service limits and throttling during high-volume tests? What proactive steps do you take?
17. Have you used AWS infrastructure-as-code (CloudFormation or Terraform) to provision and tear down performance test environments? How does this fit into your testing lifecycle?

**Monitoring & Observability**

18. How do you build a comprehensive observability stack for performance testing on AWS? How do you stitch together CloudWatch, X-Ray, and application APM tools like Dynatrace or AppDynamics?
19. Have you set up custom CloudWatch dashboards or metrics for performance testing purposes? What metrics do you typically track beyond the defaults?
20. How do you correlate AWS-side metrics (CPU, memory, ELB latency, RDS IOPS) with LoadRunner client-side metrics to build a complete picture?

**Cost & Optimization**

21. How do you balance cost vs. accuracy when running performance tests on AWS? Have you used Spot Instances for load generators? What's your fallback strategy if a Spot Instance is reclaimed mid-test?
22. How do you design tests to minimize AWS data transfer costs, especially in cross-region scenarios?

**Cloud-Native Considerations**

23. How do you performance test auto-scaling applications on AWS? How do you validate that the scaling policy itself performs correctly under load?
24. How do you test applications that use AWS-managed services like SQS, SNS, Lambda, or DynamoDB? What are the unique challenges compared to traditional app testing?
25. Have you done performance testing on containerized workloads running on ECS or EKS? How does that differ from testing on EC2?

## Strategy, Leadership & Process

26. How do you build a performance testing strategy for a large program — covering NFR definition, test scope, environment strategy, entry/exit criteria, and stakeholder reporting?
27. How do you influence development and architecture teams to act on performance findings, especially when there's pushback on timelines or priorities?
28. How have you integrated performance testing into a CI/CD pipeline? What thresholds and gates did you define, and how did you handle failures?
29. How do you mentor or upskill junior performance engineers? What does good performance engineering practice look like to you?
30. Have you contributed to NFR (Non-Functional Requirements) definition workshops? How do you challenge or validate requirements that seem unrealistic?

## Red Flag Indicators to Watch For

- Can only describe tool steps but struggles to explain **why** decisions were made
- Has never done **root cause analysis** beyond surface-level metrics
- No experience **influencing teams** or driving fixes — purely execution focused
- Cannot speak to **test environment strategy** or has always relied on others to set it up
- No awareness of **cloud cost management** in testing contexts
